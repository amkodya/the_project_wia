Catskill Chill

Hancock, NY

Incredibly beautiful venue with carefully curated national and local bands and live acts. The camping includes beautiful scenery within a historic campground by a beautiful lake! Some bands that have headlined Catskill Chill includes Umphreys Mcgee, Yonder Mountain String Band, Moe., and the Meter Men

All Good Festival

Thornton, Oh

An amazing festival with about 50,000 in attendance each year. Lineup each year includes Grateful Dead and jam band related band and acts. Some prevous years have included Further, Bob Weir, Phil Lesh, Dark Star Orchestra, STS9, and Pretty Lights

Sterling Stage

Sterling, NY

A small festival in the heart of Oswego county in upstate, NY. Featuring local bands and at least one national act.

Moe.Down

Turin, NY

Festival by the jam band Moe. This festival is located in Turin, NY and features at least 6 sets from Moe. plus other awesome jam bands.

Camp Bisco

Mariaville, NY

Festival brought to you by the jamtronica band the Disco Biscuits. The festival includes many electronic music acts and jam bands. The Disco Biscuits play at least 6 sets throughout the festival. Recently, the festival has moved to Montage Mountain in PA.

The Laughing Academy

Laurens, NY

A small festival put on my local area DJ. The festival includes several bands and several djs all local.
